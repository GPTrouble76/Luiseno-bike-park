About Us

Luiseno Bike Park hosts San Diego’s only year-round shuttle access downhill bike park with a unique backcountry flow buried in the foothills of the La Jolla Indian Reservation.

Inspired by the Luiseño people’s rich history and connection to the land, we are a unique mountain biking destination dedicated to fostering a love for the outdoors and the exhilarating sport of mountain biking.

Spread across diverse and rugged terrain, Luiseno Bike Park offers an expansive network of trails designed for riders of all skill levels.
